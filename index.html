About

Built for the dancefloor

Shredy is a selector and blender at heart — digging deep across house, disco, acid, electro, minimal and techno to build sets that move with intention. Rather than chase quick switches, he leans into long, seamless blends that keep momentum building, reading the room and guiding the energy from warm-up to peak time. Born and raised in Los Angeles with Guatemalan & Salvadoran roots, that sensitivity to flow and feel runs through every set.

Shredy's history in LA's underground runs deep — he co-founded the party series Penthouse Boogie and the project Fragments LA, both of which helped book and platform some of the city's best house & disco talent. Today he's channeling that experience as a resident DJ for House//Minimal LA, bringing his selector's ear and blend-driven style to one of LA's go-to minimal house nights.
